VectraList
Productivity with Direction.
VectraList is a high-performance, extensive task management application built to give your daily workflow true momentum. Moving beyond the limitations of standard checklists, VectraList introduces powerful data organization and bulk-editing capabilities, acting as a true command center for your professional and personal projects.

Built on a robust stack powered by React on the frontend and Express on the backend, VectraList is engineered for speed, flexibility, and absolute control over your time.

🚀 Key Features
Dynamic Categories (Workspaces): Organize your life into distinct silos. Separate your dev work, personal goals, and side hustles with dedicated, color-coded categories that filter out the noise.

Granular Status Tracking: Tasks rarely exist in just "Done" or "Not Done." VectraList utilizes customizable statuses (e.g., Backlog, In Progress, Blocked, Review, Completed) to accurately reflect your real-world progress.

Full CRUD Operations: A seamless, intuitive interface lets you Create, Read, Update, and Delete tasks instantly. Backed by an Express REST API, your data synchronizes fluidly without interrupting your flow.

Power Multi-Select: Stop clicking twenty times to clean up your board. Use multi-select to archive, delete, recategorize, or change the status of dozens of tasks simultaneously.

💻 The Tech Stack
Frontend: React (State-driven UI, component-based architecture for real-time reactivity)

Backend: Express (Lightweight, ultra-fast routing, and robust RESTful API endpoints)

Why "Vectra"?
In physics, a vector represents both direction and magnitude. VectraList doesn’t just store your tasks; it gives them direction, helping you understand not just what you need to do, but where your energy is going.
